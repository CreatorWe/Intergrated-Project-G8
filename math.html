What is a linear and a quadratic equation?

They are 2 different types of mathematical graphs. They help show the trends and statistics of multiple different topics. They also show the profits of a produc or a speed of a object. They are used everyday either by the common person, or by big corporations. Let's talk about them!

Linear Equations

Linear Equations

A Linear Equation, when plotted on a graph, is a straight line. The standard form of a linear equation in one variable is of the form ax + b = 0. Here, x is a variable, and a and b are constants. While the standard form of a linear equation in two variables is of the form ax + by = c. Here, x and y are variables, and a, b and c are constants. X and Y are also representing the x and y coordinates on the plane/graph.

Solutions: Tables of Values

A easy and good solution is using the table of values method. It is where you would make x = -3, -2, -1, 0, 1, 2, 3 on a table (less or more depending on the graph) in the equation, then solve the equation for y.
Example: x – 2y = -1 (x = 3)
3 – 2y = -1
-2y = -1 -3
-2y/-2 = -4/-2
y = 2
You are to repeat this, completing the entire table. Then with the new coordinates you have, you can plot it on a graph, and it will come out as a straight line.

Solutions: X and Y Intercept

Another solution is the x and y intercept. This is slightly easier and takes less time than the table of values. It is were you would find the intercept; the point which the line would touch the x or y part. To do this, you are to replace the x with 0, then after solving that, you are to do this again but replace y with 0.
Example: 3x + 1 = -5y (x = 0)
3(0) + 1 = -5y
1 = -5y

Quadratic Equations

Quadratic Equations

A quadratic equation, when plotted on a graph, comes out as a curve or known as a parabola. In a quadratic graph, there is the Axis of Symmetry, Maximum, Minimum, and Vertex. Axis of Symmetry is a line that goes through the middle of the parabola. Vertex is the tip of a parabola. It can either be maximum or minimum of a graph depending where it is pointing on. For example, if it is pointing down, the vertex is the minimum point. If it is pointing up, the vertex is the maximum.

Solutions: Table of Values

The solution table of values can also work on a quadratic equation. Just do the same thing, = -3, -2, -1, 0, 1, 2, 3 on a table (less or more depending on the graph) in the equation, then solve the equation for y.
Example: 3x + 1 = -5y (x = 0)
y = 25-2(-1)-2(-12)
y = 25+2-2
y = 25
You are to repeat this, completing the entire table. Then with the new coordinates you have, you can plot it on a graph, and it will come out as a curve or a parabola.
